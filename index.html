Meet Robert

A Life of Service, Leadership & Storytelling

Robert W. Starnes is an author of three books and an engaging keynote speaker who captivates audiences through humor, inspiration, and unforgettable storytelling. Drawing from a distinguished 35-year career in law enforcement and diplomacy, Robert shares extraordinary real-life experiences that offer practical lessons in leadership, resilience, service, and the power of human connection.

His remarkable career includes serving as a security attaché during a civil war and coup d'état, helping disrupt three planned terrorist attacks, serving on the first U.S. diplomatic envoy to return to Libya, leading the investigation into a chemical terrorist threat targeting golfer Tiger Woods, rescuing two-dozen abused juveniles from the jungles of Samoa, dismantling a German mafia cell operating in Texas, and navigating countless other high-stakes assignments around the world.

Through a faith-based perspective, Robert demonstrates how simple acts of kindness, courage, and integrity can transform individuals, strengthen communities, and even influence nations.
